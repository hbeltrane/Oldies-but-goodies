About

This website was created by Hugo Beltran Escarraga (C0845680) as my 2022 Winter's term project for CSD-1103: Front End Web Development I course in CSAM program at Lambton College in Mississauga. Its purpose is to display information about the products and services for a fictional company named Oldies But Goodies, whose mission is to restore cars and to offer them to its customers under different alternatives.

I am a computer programmer from Colombia, videogames enthusiast and passionate about cars. This website is the result of getting together information from my hobbies. The pictures of junk cars were taken from real images in the internet, whilst the pictures of restored cars were taken from the PC version of Microsoft Studiosâ€™ Forza Horizon 4 videogame. Information and specifications were taken from both Wikipedia and Forza Horizon 4.

I decided to use Forza Horizon videogame because it is the release that has the highest quantity of content to the date. It also offers a high quality and realistic graphics engine to deliver accurate 3D models that match the images of real junk cars, allowing me to display them in a before/after layout. Macromedia Fireworks was used to create textures and optimize the pictures. The coding of the website was done with Notepad++.

The logo, banner, layout and text were created for this project by me; all credit for images, brands, names, information, etc. used in this website goes to their respective owners.
